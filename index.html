Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a Data Analyst proficient in Excel, VBA, SQL, R, Tableau, BigQuery, and always learning more!

I share some of my data analyst projects here, as well as some professional resources including links to my resume, awarded certificates, PublicTableau profile, and more.

As an employee with the task of helping create databases for several different employers, I have lots of hands-on experience creating and using databases. I not only understand what real-world data looks like, but how to clean it up and properly format it for its particular and unique use in business applications and data analysis. After collecting, cleaning, and generally making the data usable, my previous roles shifted into management, analysis, and generally running reports on the information stored in the database. I also made automated Excel sheets using formulas and VBA to reducing bureaucratic paperwork, data collection, and report generation.

My Google Data Analytics Certificate also helped me to further expand my abilities and understanding of what skills, information, and data could be useful for companies, clients, and employers, and how to find, collect, clean, analyze, and present that data.
